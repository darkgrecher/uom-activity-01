UNIVERSITY OF MORATUWA

The University of Moratuwa is a leading technological university in Sri Lanka. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in various fields such as engineering, architecture, IT, business management, and science. The university has a strong emphasis on research and innovation, and its graduates are highly sought after by local and international employers. The campus is located in Moratuwa, a suburb of Colombo, and is known for its modern facilities and lively student community.
